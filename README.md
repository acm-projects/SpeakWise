<h1 align="center"> SpeakWise </h1>

<div align="center"> Have you ever struggled with public speaking or been nervous about acing that upcoming interview? SpeakWise is your ultimate companion for mastering the art of speech and interview preparation! Record your speech effortlessly within the app and watch as SpeakWise analyzes your tone, pace, and counts your filler words. Additionally, experience mock interviews catered to your job description with personalized interview questions and response analysis. Join SpeakWise ‚Äì where your speaking troubles end and communication begins! </div>


## MVP üåü
- User Account
- Record speech
- Analyze tone using sentiment analysis
- Analyze pace using WPM algorithm
- Count filler words
- AI generated interview questions based on job description

## Stretch Goals ü™ê
- Daily challenges to reinforce speaking skills
- Social Aspect (sharing stats such as number of filler words)
- Analyze additional aspects of speech or interview responses (clarity, emotion, etc.)
- Display statistics related to speech improvement (how the amount of filler words changes, etc.)
- Give tips on how to improve speech based on analysis
